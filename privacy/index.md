# ScoreHabit Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 2026

## Introduction

ScoreHabit ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how the ScoreHabit mobile application handles your data.

## Offline-Only Application

ScoreHabit is a fully offline application. All data is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. **No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.**

## No Account Required

ScoreHabit does not require account creation, login, or any form of authentication. There is no username, email, or password collection.

## Information Stored on Your Device

### Practice Data (stored locally only)

- Musical works and repertoire information you enter
- Practice session data (time, measures studied, notes)
- Progress statistics calculated from your sessions

### Profile Information (stored locally only)

- Name, instruments, and ensembles you choose to enter
- App preferences (language, theme)

All of this information is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted externally.

## No Data Collection

ScoreHabit does **not** collect, transmit, or share:

- Personal information
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Device identifiers
- Location data
- Any data whatsoever to external servers

## No Third-Party Services

ScoreHabit does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or cloud storage providers.

## No Internet Access

ScoreHabit does not require or use an internet connection. The app functions entirely offline.

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all ScoreHabit data at any time by:

- Clearing the app's data through your device's Settings
- Uninstalling the application

## Children's Privacy

ScoreHabit is suitable for users of all ages. Since the app does not collect or transmit any personal information, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
